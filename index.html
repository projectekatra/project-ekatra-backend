Project Ekatra

An intiative to collect all educational contents available freely on internet at one place for ease of use and to find easily. There are many hidden treasure out there on Internet waiting for students to get explroe, learn and get most of it. So why not create a map for all those treasures at one place. Here you can be wizards who know the map or the treasure hunter who needs a way. For learners by Learners
